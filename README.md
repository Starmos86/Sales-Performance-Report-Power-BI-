## 🧮 Dimensions & Metrics Overview

### 🔹 Dimension Filters
- Region
- Customer Segment
- Product Category
- Product Sub-Category

### 📈 Performance Metrics
- Quantity Sold  
- Sales Revenue  
- Profit  
- Discount Given

### ⚙️ Efficiency Metrics
- Profit Margin  
- Sales per Customer  
- Orders per Customer  
- Quantity per Customer  
- Profit per Order
